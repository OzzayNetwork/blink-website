How do I recover a lost password?
If you forget your password, noneed to panic from the Forgot password link on the app's login page, you will be directed on how you ill recover your password.
How can I log in for the first time as a guardian?
Once your gurdian account is created by the school via the portal, the gurdian will receive a text message that contains a download link to their account after which they may start setting up their account.
Can I manage more than one child from one application??
Yes. from a single guardian app, a parent can add or remove a child from their list of guardians. A guardian will easily have access to any of the childs they are assigned to.
Can one child be assigned to multiple guardians?
Yes, this is very possible. A blinker(Student) may be assigned to more than one guardian with the concent of both the school and the gurdian originally registered to. From there the newly added guardan has access to all functionalities of the app.
